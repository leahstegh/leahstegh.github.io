My favorite sports

My favorite sport to play is lacrosse. I really like to play it because it encorperates a lot of muscles and movements, making it very fun to play. I also really like to watch lacrosse. I think that watching lacrosse is one of the best ways to learn because you can see the moves that the professional athletes perform. I frequently go to Northwestern girls lacrosse games. They are very fast paced and exciting because Northwestern has such an excellent lacrosse program. Northwestern is often ranked as having the best D1 lacrosse team, which explains why the games are so cool to watch.

Quote from Charlotte North

“Lacrosse was a place where we got to be together, let loose and do what we love,” North said. “It was the best three hours of the day. it was like every day was Christmas Day.”
