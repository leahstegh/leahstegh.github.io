Cheerleading

What Cheer Means to me

Cheer is something I have been very passionate about for as long as I remember. The mix of physical and mental requirements have always kept me engaged. I was on various competitive club teams for 3 years. Then, I decided to stop in order to be able to cheer for Lane Tech. After much hard work, the Lane Tech Varsity team won City in January of this year! Being on the Lane Tech cheer team has not only made me a more valuable athlete, but taught me how to communicate my needs better. It taught me to be a more active member of the team and to share my views in order to create changes.

What Winning City Meant to Us

This season for Lane Cheer was long, fun, and exciting. The maintained a very postive mindset throughout the season despite the long and hard practices. Most weeks, our team practiced 5 times a week in hopes of bringing home a city championship title. It meant a lot to us, and we were dedeicated to completing our goal. The Lane team hadn't won city since 2021, 4 years ago. After countless hours practicing, it finally was the big day. Our hard work payed off and we preformed a near perfect routine. The judges loved it and we ended up outpreforming all of the other teams, making us the 2025 CPS large varisty champions!
